The Approach

A structured path to
real improvement

01

Build a solid opening repertoire

One reliable system as White, one solid response to each of 1.e4 and 1.d4. Consistency matters more than variety at 1500. Every opening tab shows the plans behind the moves.

02

Understand structures, not lines

The middlegame section teaches the three structural concepts that drive most positions — outposts, pawn breaks, and the IQP. Once you recognise these, the right plan becomes visible without deep calculation.

03

Solve tactical patterns daily

The puzzle bank rotates through 15 positions at three difficulty levels. At 1500, tactical awareness is the single biggest source of rating points.

04

Convert your winning endgames

The three essential endgames — king and pawn opposition, Lucena, Philidor — cover almost every endgame you’ll face. Master them and stop throwing away won games.
